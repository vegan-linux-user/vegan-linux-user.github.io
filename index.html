Metroid Remake

I developed a remake of Metroid using Unity and C#. In this project, I focused on creating seamless player movement and animations, implementing sound design, and designing levels that provide an engaging user experience.

Some of the features I worked on include a health system, knockback and damage interactions, player invincibility frames, and powerups/collectables. I also implemented doors and room transitions, camera following and bounding, and a custom magnet beam mechanic using Unity/C# features like coroutines and the singleton design pattern.
